Files named AnnualMax_H_(station)_(model).txt:
- Annual maximum sea ice thickness in 4 coastal stations (Kemi, Kalajoki, Sälgrund, Kylmäpihlaja)
- For hindcast (HC): data format is [year, thickness (m)]
- For ensembles (ABD002 and ABD005): data format is [year, thickness (m) in A, thickness (m) in B, thickness (m) in D]
- Data of ABD001 (years 1976-2005) is included in files of ABD002 and ABD005. I.e. first 30 years of ABD002 and ABD005 are identical.
- Year denotes the year when ice season ends. For example, for the maximum thickness of ice season 1980-1981 year is 1981.
